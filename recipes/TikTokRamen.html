TikTok Ramen

This dish is the perfect super-quick and easy to make dish that will keep you coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of instant ramen
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 lightly beaten egg
  • 1 tsp of everything bagel spice

Instructions

  1. First cook the ramen according to the package instructions
  2. While the ramen is cooking, in a seperate pan heat up 1 tbsp of butter over medium heat.
  3. Once the butter is melted, add in 1 tsp of minced garlic and 1/4 tsp of red pepper flakes
  4. Cook the garlic until its soft and then stir in 1 tsp brown sugar and 1 tbsp soy sauce
  5. Then add the drained ramen noodles into the sauce and stir it around to coat the noodles
  6. Turn the heat up slightly and push the noodles to one side
  7. Add the lightly beaten egg into the space beside the noodles and scramble until its done
  8. Once the egg is done, add the ramen into your serving dish and sprinkle with the everything bagel seasoning
  9. The last step is to enjoy your creation!
